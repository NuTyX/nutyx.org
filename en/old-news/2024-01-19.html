Latest news 19-01-2024

NuTyX 24.01.0 available with cards 2.7.4

Spiky and me are happy to announce the new version of NuTyX 24.01.0 and cards 2.7.4 .

Fixed bugs

The linux-firmware is very big (more then 1 GB). It must be splited. Fixed

Updated packages

SysV in 3.06 and systemd in 253.0 .

The xorg-server graphics server version 21.1.10, the Mesa 3D library in 23.2.1, Gtk4 4.12.4 and Qt 6.6.1.

  • The nvidia driver is update to version 535.154.05 .
  • The legacy driver 470.xx series in version 470.223.02 .
  • The legacy driver 390.xx series in version 390.157.13 .

The python interpreter is updated to version 3.11.6.

The XFCE desktop environment is updated to version 4.18.3.

The MATE desktop environment is a 1.26.2 version .

The GNOME desktop environment is also updated to version 45.2

The KDE desktop environment is available in Plasma 5.27.10, Framework 5.114.0 and applications in 23.08.4.

Available browsers are: Firefox 121.0.1, Epiphany 45.1, etc

Many desktop applications have been updated as well like Telegram-desktop 4.12.2, Thunderbird 115.6.1, Scribus 1.5.8, Libreoffice 7.6.3.2, Gimp 2.10.36, etc.

Core NuTyX ships with Long Term Support (LTS) kernels: 4.19.305, 5.4.267, 5.10.208, 5.15.147, 6.1.73 and 6.6.12 and the latest stable version 6.7.0.

12 desktops are now available in: XORG, OPENBOX, LXDE, XFCE4, JWM, LXQT, ENLIGHTENMENT, MATE, CINNAMON, BUDGIE, GNOME, KDE5.

They are available on the download page.
